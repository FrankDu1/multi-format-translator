# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

作为成员、贡献者和领导者，我们承诺让社区中的每个人都能获得无骚扰的体验，
无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、种族、性别特征、性别认同和表达、
经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向如何。

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
有助于创造积极环境的行为示例包括：

* Using welcoming and inclusive language / 使用欢迎和包容的语言
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences / 尊重不同的观点和经验
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism / 优雅地接受建设性批评
* Focusing on what is best for the community / 关注对社区最有利的事情
* Showing empathy towards other community members / 对其他社区成员表现出同理心

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
不可接受的行为示例包括：

* The use of sexualized language or imagery / 使用性化的语言或图像
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks / 恶意评论、侮辱性言论以及个人或政治攻击
* Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
* Publishing others' private information without explicit permission / 未经明确许可发布他人的私人信息
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate / 其他可被合理认为不适当的行为

## Enforcement / 执行

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at
[offerupup@offerupup.cn].

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

可以通过 [offerupup@offerupup.cn] 向负责执行的社区领导者报告
滥用、骚扰或其他不可接受的行为。

所有投诉都将得到及时和公正的审查和调查。
